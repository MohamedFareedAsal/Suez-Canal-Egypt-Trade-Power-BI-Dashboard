# Suez Canal & Egypt Trade Power BI Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Egypt's economic indicators and Suez Canal performance between 2015 and 2024.

The dashboard combines multiple datasets to provide insights into canal revenues, foreign trade, exchange rates, inflation, foreign reserves, oil prices, and major economic events.

## Features

- Interactive KPI Cards
- Revenue Trend Analysis
- USD/EGP Exchange Rate Analysis
- Foreign Trade Balance
- Foreign Reserves Analysis
- Oil Price vs Canal Traffic Correlation
- Interactive Slicers
- Event Timeline

---

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Power BI Visualizations

---

## Dataset

- Suez Canal Revenue
- Ships Passing Through the Canal
- Egypt Foreign Trade
- USD/EGP Exchange Rate
- Inflation Rate
- Foreign Reserves
- Brent Oil Prices
- Major Economic Events

---

## Key Insights

- Canal revenue reached its highest level in 2023.
- Revenue declined sharply during the Red Sea crisis in 2024.
- Egypt experienced multiple currency devaluations.
- Foreign reserves recovered to record levels in 2024.
- Brent oil prices show a positive relationship with canal traffic.
